Hello! I'm Nickolas Moreira, Welcome to My Portfolio!

About Me

I am currently a student at CPCC (Central Piedmont Community College), where I am pursuing an Associate in Arts degree. I plan to transfer to UNCC (University of North Carolina at Charlotte) in the future. My passion for development began in middle school when my sister introduced me to coding through classes focused on HTML and CSS. Since then, I've developed my skills by practicing with resources like Codecademy and freeCodeCamp.

What Are My Goals?

I am driven by the goal of breaking into the tech industry and landing my first role as a junior developer or internship. My long-term ambition is to become a full-stack developer and eventually work at one of the top tech companies, often referred to as the FAANG giants. Above all, I aim to add value wherever I work by being reliable, adaptable, and committed to continuous learning.

What Do I Do Outside of Code?

In addition to coding, I have a variety of hobbies that keep me balanced. Fitness is a big part of my life; I regularly go to the gym to lift weights and stay in shape, with the bench press being one of my favorite exercises. Art is another creative outlet I enjoy. I’ve been drawing since I was young and aim to complete my first sketchbook. I also practice aquascaping, which is one of my most therapeutic hobbies. Watching my aquarium plants sway and observing my fish, shrimp, and snails interact in the tank offers a calming escape from everyday life. On a quiet Sunday evening, you’ll likely find me in front of my tanks, immersed in the peaceful atmosphere I've created.
